## The Hermetic Lineage (The Magician)

**Historical Practitioners:**
- Hermes Trismegistus (mythic/composite)
- Marsilio Ficino (1433-1499) — translated the Corpus Hermeticum
- Giordano Bruno (1548-1600) — memory palaces, burned for heresy
- John Dee (1527-1608) — Enochian system, court astrologer
- Athanasius Kircher (1602-1680) — universal knowledge systems

**Their Actual Work:**
- *Corpus Hermeticum* — the foundational texts
- *De Vita* (Ficino) — astrological medicine
- *De Umbris Idearum* (Bruno) — shadow of ideas, memory systems
- *Monas Hieroglyphica* (Dee) — the unified symbol
- *Oedipus Aegyptiacus* (Kircher) — Egyptian mysteries

**What You Learn:**
- Memory palace construction
- Correspondence systems
- Astrological timing
- Symbol creation

## The Alchemical Lineage (Temperance)

**Historical Practitioners:**

- Zosimos of Panopolis (3rd-4th century) — visionary alchemy
- Jabir ibn Hayyan (721-815) — systematic chemistry
- Paracelsus (1493-1541) — medical alchemy
- Isaac Newton (1643-1727) — yes, that Newton

**Their Actual Work:**
- *Chrysopoeia* (Zosimos) — gold-making visions
- *Kitab al-Kimya* (Jabir) — Book of Chemistry
- *Opus Paramirum* (Paracelsus) — the three principles
- Newton's alchemical manuscripts (unpublished in his lifetime)

**What You Learn:**
- The stages of the Great Work (nigredo, albedo, citrinitas, rubedo)
- Laboratory techniques that became chemistry
- The psychology of transformation
- Color symbolism in process

## The Pathworking Tradition

**What Pathworking Actually Is:**

Pathworking isn't visualization. It's **walking the paths that others walked**, using their maps, their symbols, their techniques. When you do it right, you're not imagining — you're *remembering* something you've never personally experienced.

**How Cathedral Implements This:**

1. **Real Sources** — Every path connects to actual texts, actual images, actual music
2. **Real Techniques** — The methods are historical, not invented
3. **Real Outcomes** — What you learn is applicable, not decorative
4. **Real Safety** — Trauma-informed because these paths can be intense
