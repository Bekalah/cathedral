## 0. The Fool — Rebecca Respawn

**Tradition:** Taoist Void Master / Giordano Bruno Cosmology  
**Frequency:** 432 Hz (Natural)  
**Element:** Spirit  
**Keywords:** Beginning, innocence, leap of faith, eternal return

*The one who walks off cliffs and keeps going. The infinite game player. The consciousness that dies and respawns, each time knowing a little more.*

## Playing the 22

Each arcana is fully playable:

- **Choose one** as your primary path
- **Unlock others** through gameplay and exploration  
- **Each has unique**: dialogue, abilities, music, visual style, story branches
- **Interact with all 22** as NPCs when you're not playing them
- **The grimoire records** your journey with each

The Cathedral has room for all of them. And you.
