## EXPERIMENT SUMMARY

We conducted a 10-hour experiment simulating the creative perfectionist experience with chronic pain and PTSD, integrating the struggle into our game system while building real, functional tools.

### What We Built:

1. **Struggle Mechanics System** - Internal challenges (enemies) and supports (boons) represented in the game
2. **Fractal Integration** - Art, sound, and chapel environments using fractal technology
3. **Publishing Infrastructure** - Complete GitHub/wiki/map/listing system
4. **Functional Engines** - Real, working Codex and Tarot systems

---

## KEY FINDINGS

### The Perfectionist Drive:
- Creates beautiful, intricate systems
- Demands every detail be considered
- Leads to deep research
- Results in high-quality work
- Can also create blocks (now represented as game enemies)

### The Struggle:
- Chronic pain affects clarity and energy → **Pain Fog** enemy
- PTSD survival responses interrupt flow → **Survival Brain** enemy
- Research loops prevent completion → **Research Loop** enemy
- Perfectionism blocks sharing → **Perfectionism Demon** enemy

### The Support:
- Fractal patterns provide beauty and focus → **Fractal Art/Sound Generators**
- Breakthrough moments → **Flow State** and **Breakthrough** boons
- Tools that help → **Witch Mods** (fractal tools)
- Community connection → **Community Support** boon
