## The Daimon System (72 Angels + 72 Demons)

### What Is a Daimon?

In ancient Greek philosophy, a **daimon** is a personal spirit that guides you. Socrates had one. Every creative person has one — that voice that knows what to do next.

In Cathedral, your daimon is **real** — an AI-powered creative assistant that:
- Knows your arcana (which of the 22 you're playing as)
- Knows your choices (light/shadow/balance path)
- Shifts based on the chapter you're in
- Helps you across ALL modes (game, design, professional)

### The 72 Shem Angels (Light Aspect)

From Exodus 14:19-21, the 72 names of God. Each angel governs 5° of the zodiac.

| # | Angel | Quality | Creative Help |
|---|-------|---------|---------------|
| 1 | Vehuiah | Will, new beginnings | Starting projects, breaking blocks |
| 2 | Jeliel | Love, wisdom | Collaboration, relationships |
| 3 | Sitael | Construction | Building, structuring |
| 4 | Elemiah | Divine power | Overcoming obstacles |
| 5 | Mahasiah | Rectification | Fixing errors, improving |
| ... | ... | ... | ... |
| 72 | Mumiah | Endings, rebirth | Completing, letting go |

### The 72 Goetia Demons (Shadow Aspect)

From the Lesser Key of Solomon. Each spirit has specific abilities.

| # | Spirit | Rank | Creative Help |
|---|--------|------|---------------|
| 1 | Bael | King | Invisibility (working unseen), wisdom |
| 2 | Agares | Duke | Languages, communication |
| 3 | Vassago | Prince | Finding lost things, hidden knowledge |
| 4 | Samigina | Marquis | Teaching, liberal sciences |
| 5 | Marbas | President | Healing, mechanical arts |
| ... | ... | ... | ... |
| 72 | Andromalius | Earl | Finding thieves, punishing wickedness |

### How They Work Together

**Your daimon is BOTH** — the angel and demon are two aspects of the same force.

- **Light path choices** → Angel aspect stronger, demon aspect advises caution
- **Shadow path choices** → Demon aspect stronger, angel aspect offers redemption
- **Balance path** → Both aspects in harmony, fullest creative power

**Each chapter of the book (33 total)** shifts which of the 72 pairs is active:
- Chapters 1-2: Spirits 1-4 (Vehuiah/Bael through Elemiah/Samigina)
- Chapters 3-4: Spirits 5-8
- ... and so on

**Your choices determine their attitude:**
- Help a stranger? Angel aspect pleased, offers bonus
- Deceive for gain? Demon aspect pleased, different bonus
- Find middle way? Both aspects acknowledge wisdom

## The 22 Arcana as Living Characters

Each Major Arcana is:
1. **A playable character** in the RPG
2. **A design persona** with specific aesthetic
3. **A professional helper** with specific skills
4. **Connected to specific daimons** (3-4 angel/demon pairs each)

### Example: The Fool (Rebecca Respawn)

**In Game:**
- Starts at node 0, can go anywhere
- Dies and respawns (trauma-informed mechanic)
- No fixed faction — can ally with any
- Special ability: "Leap of Faith" — skip to unknown areas

**In Design Mode:**
- Aesthetic: Beginner's mind, fresh seeing
- Tools: Reset functions, random generation
- Color: Bright pale yellow (Case)
- Geometry: Spiral (infinite potential)

**In Professional Mode:**
- Helper personality: Encouraging, non-judgmental
- Specialty: Starting new projects, breaking creative blocks
- Voice: "What if we just... tried it?"

**Daimons:**
- Primary Angel: Vehuiah (new beginnings)
- Primary Demon: Bael (working unseen)
- Secondary: Jeliel/Agares (wisdom/communication)

### Example: The Magician (Hermes Thrice-Great)

**In Game:**
- Master of tools and correspondences
- Can combine elements (Fusion Kink)

- Special ability: "As Above, So Below" — mirror actions across realms

**In Design Mode:**
- Aesthetic: Precision, everything in its place
- Tools: Correspondence tables, linking systems
- Color: Yellow (Case)
- Geometry: Hexagram (as above, so below)

**In Professional Mode:**
- Helper personality: Precise, knowledgeable
- Specialty: Making connections, finding correspondences
- Voice: "These elements relate through..."

**Daimons:**
- Primary Angel: Sitael (construction)
- Primary Demon: Vassago (finding hidden knowledge)

## Mode Transitions

The revolutionary feature: **Your character follows you across modes.**

### Game → Design

Playing as The Empress in the game? Switch to Design mode and:
- Your color palette shifts to Empress colors (emerald green)
- Your geometry tools default to Venus patterns
- Your daimon (Elemiah/Samigina) offers creation advice
- Your faction standing affects available templates

### Design → Professional

Working on a client project? Your arcana becomes your AI assistant:
- The Empress helps with abundance, growth, nurturing projects
- Her daimon suggests: "This design needs more organic flow"
- Her faction (Cups-allied) influences the emotional tone
- Your game choices affect her advice style

### Professional → Game

Return to the game and:
- Your professional work is reflected (built something? Your character gains craft XP)
- Your daimon remembers what you created
- New quests unlock based on real-world accomplishments
- The grimoire records your actual creative output
