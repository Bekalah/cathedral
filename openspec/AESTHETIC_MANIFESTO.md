## The Monas Hieroglyphica Principle

John Dee created a single symbol that contained:
- The Sun ☉
- The Moon ☽
- The Elements (Fire △, Earth ▽, Air △̅, Water ▽̅)
- The Cross ✝
- Aries ♈

**One symbol. All knowledge.**

Cathedral follows this principle:
- Every element serves multiple purposes
- Every design choice connects to correspondences
- Nothing is decorative — everything is functional
- The whole system is one unified symbol

### Implementation

```
The Cathedral itself IS a Monas:

         ☉ (Crown/Kether)
         |
    ☽────┼────★ (Pillars)
         |
    △────┼────▽ (Elements)
         |
    ♈────┴────♎ (Zodiac)
         
Every screen, every interface, every interaction
reflects this unified structure.
```

## The McQueen Principle

Alexander McQueen understood:
- **Beauty can be disturbing**
- **Craft is everything** — every stitch matters
- **Emotion drives design** — not trends
- **The weird is the point** — not something to hide
- **Nature is dark** — skulls, bones, feathers, decay
- **Theatricality serves meaning** — not spectacle for its own sake

### What This Means for Cathedral

**Color:**
- Not bright primaries
- Deep, complex tones
- Burnished metals (not shiny chrome)
- Blacks with undertones (warm black, cool black, violet black)
- Golds that feel ancient, not new

**Typography:**
- Bodoni Moda — sharp serifs, high contrast, theatrical
- Cormorant Garamond — elegant, historical weight
- NO: Inter, Roboto, system fonts
- Letters as objects, not just information carriers

**Layout:**
- Asymmetric but balanced
- Negative space as design element
- Nothing centered unless intentionally ceremonial
- Depth through layering, not drop shadows

**Animation:**
- Slow, deliberate movements
- Easing that feels like breathing
- Nothing bounces
- Things emerge, unfold, reveal
- McQueen's runway: models moved like they were haunted

**Imagery:**
- Sacred geometry that feels discovered, not generated
- Textures from real materials (vellum, metal, glass)
- Depth and dimension
- Dark backgrounds with luminous elements
- NO: flat illustrations, cartoon characters, stock photos

## The Automatic Protection Principle

The system protects without asking:

### Privacy by Design
- **No user data leaves the device** — all AI runs locally (Ollama)
- **No tracking** — no analytics that identify users
- **No accounts required** — play anonymously if you want
- **No cloud dependency** — works offline
- **Your grimoire is YOURS** — stored locally, exportable, deletable

### Automatic Debugging
- **Self-healing builds** — CI/CD catches errors before deploy
- **Type safety** — TypeScript strict mode everywhere
- **Pre-commit hooks** — bad code can't enter the repo
- **Graceful degradation** — if something fails, the rest works

### Security Without Friction
- **No passwords to remember** — local-first means no auth needed
- **No API keys exposed** — everything runs on your machine
- **No payment info** — nothing to steal because nothing costs money
- **Open source** — you can verify every line of code

## The Weird

Cathedral embraces the weird because:

1. **The traditions are weird** — Dee talked to angels, Crowley channeled Aiwass, Carrington painted her kitchen as an alchemical lab

2. **Real creativity is weird** — McQueen sent models down runways with wolves, covered in butterflies, wrapped in bandages

3. **The weird is honest** — Pretending everything is normal is the lie

4. **Weird attracts weird** — The right people will find this and feel home

### What "Weird" Means Here

- A daimon (angel/demon pair) that talks to you while you design
- A grimoire that writes itself as you play
- Synthesizers tuned to frequencies from medieval manuscripts
- Color palettes derived from Kabbalistic correspondences
- A game where dying is the point (Rebecca Respawn)

### What "Weird" Does NOT Mean

- Random for the sake of random
- Edgy for shock value
- Incomprehensible on purpose
- Ugly as an aesthetic choice
- Broken as a feature
